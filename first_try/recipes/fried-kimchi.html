Fried Kimchi

Description

Kimchi is a popular Korean side dish featuring fermented cabbage, and now we fry it here.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kimchi, chopped into bite-sized pieces, or to taste
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil, or to taste
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons white vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar, or to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a nonstick skillet to medium high heat.
  2. Add kimchi
  3. Saute until fragrant and yellow, about 5 minutes.
  4. Add sesame oil; cook until kimchi is coated, 2 to 3 minutes.
  5. Pour in vinegar to coat the bottom of the skillet.
  6. Increase heat slightly; bring vinegar to a boil.
  7. Add sugar. Reduce heat to low; cook until sugar dissolves and kimchi is browned, about 3 minutes.
