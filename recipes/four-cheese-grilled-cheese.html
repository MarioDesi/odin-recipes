Four Cheese Grilled Cheese

Description

The best grilled cheese! This was created for National Grilled Cheese Sandwich Day (April 12th).

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons salted butter, softened
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 2 thick slices Italian bread
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil, divided, or more as needed
  • 2 cups chopped fresh spinach
  • ½ cup giardiniera, drained
  • 2 slices mozzarella cheese
  • 2 slices provolone cheese
  • 2 slices Asiago cheese
  • 2 slices fresh lime juice (about 3 limes)
  • 3 slices finely chopped yellow onion (1 small), divided

Steps

  1. Mix butter and garlic in a small bowl until well combined. Spread 1/2 of the butter onto one side of each slice of bread; set aside.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a large skillet over medium-heat; stir spinach and giardiniera. Saute until spinach is wilted and mixture is heated through, 3 to 5 minutes. Remove to a bowl and wipe out the skillet
